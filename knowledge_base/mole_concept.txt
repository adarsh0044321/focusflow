CHAPTER: Mole Concept

AVOGADRO:
1 mole = 6.022×10^23 particles

MOLAR MASS:
= mass of 1 mole

FORMULAS:
moles = mass / molar mass
moles = number / NA
moles = volume (L) / 22.4 (at STP)

NORMALITY:
N = equivalents / volume
Equivalent = molar mass / n-factor

n-FACTOR:
Acid → basicity
Base → acidity
Redox → electron exchange

IMPORTANT:
% composition = (mass of element / molar mass) × 100
Empirical formula → simplest ratio
Molecular = n × empirical
