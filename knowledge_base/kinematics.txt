CHAPTER: Kinematics

BASICS:
Displacement (s), Velocity (v), Acceleration (a)

FORMULAS:
v = u + at
s = ut + (1/2)at²
v² = u² + 2as
s = ((u + v)/2)t

AVERAGE VELOCITY:
= total displacement / time

RELATIVE VELOCITY:
v_AB = v_A - v_B

PROJECTILE MOTION:
Time of flight = 2u sinθ / g
Max height = u² sin²θ / (2g)
Range = u² sin2θ / g

IMPORTANT:
Horizontal velocity constant
Vertical motion under gravity

GRAPHS:
Slope of x-t → velocity
Slope of v-t → acceleration
Area under v-t → displacement
